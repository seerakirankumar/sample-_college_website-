About Us

QIS College of Engineering and Technology was established in 1998 under the sponsorship of Sri Nidamanuri Educational Society. The institution was founded to promote quality technical education.

The college is one of the leading institutions in Prakasam District and is known for maintaining high academic standards and discipline.

It is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK), and offers various undergraduate engineering programs.

Equal importance is given to both theoretical knowledge and practical training to prepare students for successful careers.
